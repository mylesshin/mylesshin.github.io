Flow

Full-Stack Developer

Flow is my chat web app that I created in Python for the back-end and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the front-end. This project was meant to give me a lot more back-end experience since I got to use Flask, Socket-io, and MongoDB. Flow was created to allow family members and friends to message each other online in private chat rooms.

Guardian

Lead Full-Stack Developer

Guardian is a web application created to help the elderly take their medications. Since it is hard for the elderly to remember to take their medication, Guardian would send them a push notification and if they did not indicate that they have taken it yet, it would send a text to their close contacts to check up on them. As the lead full-stack developer, I led the team in the design and user interface, the Twilio message bot, the login page, and the reCaptcha implemented to prevent bot spamming. Click here to see the demo video.

Tread Lightly

Founder | App Developer | Head Editor

Tread Lightly was created in order to get Generation Z to read the news more. With most articles on other news sources, like Apple News, being well over 5 pages long, the new generation just simply doesn't read that. So, this website and app has articles under 250 words with lively words to get the new generation to read the news. Click here to see what the app looked like.
