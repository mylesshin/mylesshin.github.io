purpose

I have always loved to code. I already had a good understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to code my own website, but it was a daunting task. Having no guidelines and having the ability to do whatever I wanted was an exhilarating, but weird feeling. It lets me take full control and put my creativity and methodical approach to the test.
